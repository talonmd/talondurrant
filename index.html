About Me

Since my first class on how to use HTML, I have been hooked on development. Since then I have worked tirelessly on teaching myself development and problem solving skills, and making myself the best developer I can be. This passion has translated into me taking coursework at Utah Valley University in Web Design and Development, and that, along with my self-taught courses over the last year and a half, has made me ready to enter the workforce as a Full Stack Developer.

During my time at UVU I graduated with an A.A. in Behavioral Science, and finished all the coursework for an A.A.S. in Web Design and Development. Through that, I gained a solid understanding of UX/UI Design Principles and how to create user-focused designs. Through this process I taught myself development, delving deeply into React, Node, Express, noSQL databases (MongoDB), and additional the frameworks, libraries, and ecosystems that come along with those technologies. I have also begun learning Docker, Kubernetes, Microservices based architectures, and other technologies on the DevOps side of things.
