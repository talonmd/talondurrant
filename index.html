About Me

I am a Full Stack Developer with specialization in JavaScript. I have been developing for just under two years now. My technologies of choice have recenly been React/Redux for the front-end, and Node/Express for the back-end, as well as NoSQL database systems. I am currently close to completing a B.S. in Software Development at Western Governors University online, to allow myself to work full-time.
